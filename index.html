UFO Sightings

An unidentified flying object (UFO), more recently renamed by US officials as an unidentified aerial phenomenon (UAP),[1] is any perceived aerial phenomenon that cannot be immediately identified or explained. On investigation, most UFOs are identified as known objects or atmospheric phenomena, while a small number remain unexplained.
